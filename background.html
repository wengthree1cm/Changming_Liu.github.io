Background

I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Health Data Science, where I combine machine learning and statistical modeling with biomedical data to uncover actionable insights. My academic foundation was built at UCSD, where I worked on neuroscience-related image processing and predictive modeling using Python, OpenCV, and MATLAB.

Professionally, I’ve gained hands-on experience across both life sciences and financial analytics. At CNPC USA and BCG, I developed scalable ETL and NLP pipelines to accelerate data workflows for large genomic and geospatial datasets, while improving model interpretability and deployment efficiency. My work spans from deploying LLM-powered dashboards for CRISPR simulations to automating investor report generation using Python, SQL, and visualization tools. Earlier at CITIC Securities, I leveraged regression and Monte Carlo simulations to analyze fund risks, and implemented automated pipelines for parsing market news and macroeconomic data.

Across every role, I’ve sought to bridge data science with high-impact domains—whether that means delivering real-time dashboards in biotech, optimizing prediction models for customer churn, or enhancing infrastructure with FastAPI and cloud platforms like AWS.
